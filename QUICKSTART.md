## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working?
- Check app permissions in phone settings
- Restart the app
- Try "SCAN PRODUCT" again

### No Voice?
- Turn up device volume
- Check TTS is enabled in phone settings
- Go to Settings > Test Voice

### Recognition Failed?
- Try better lighting
- Hold phone closer (or farther)
- Make sure label is visible
- Try again with "SCAN AGAIN" button
